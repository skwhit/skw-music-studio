Meet the founder!

Spencer Whitlow is 26 years old and has had a passion for music his entire life. He plays multiple instruments and has experience in classical music, jazz, worship, improvisation, and more. He started SKW Music as a way to share his passion of music with those around him. He believes that music can influence the emotions of others in a deeply positive way. Music is the way humans communicate in a way that words, simply don't do justice to.
